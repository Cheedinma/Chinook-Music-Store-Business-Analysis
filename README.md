# Chinook-Music-Store-Business-Analysis
# Project Overview
This project performs an end-to-end relational database exploration of the Chinook digital music store using SQL on SQLite via Jupyter Notebook. The analysis uncovers key sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, and regional metrics to guide strategic decisions regarding customer retention and business growth
# Tech Stack & Tools
 Environment: Jupyter Notebook
 
 Database Engine: SQLite / SQL
 
 Libraries: Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
# Business Questions 
Top-Selling Artists and sales trend over time

Customer Purchase Patterns: Segment customers based on purchase behavior and identify key characteristics of high-value customers.

Genre Popularity: Determine the most popular music genres and analyze the change in genre popularity over different time periods.

Sales Over Time: Analyze monthly and yearly sales trends, including seasonal effects and significant sales events.

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): Calculate the lifetime value of customers based on their purchase history and provide recommendations for improving customer retention.

# Insights
Sales Insight: Top five artists based on sales are Iron Madien, U2, Metallica, Led Zeppelin and Lost. Rock remained the most popular genre over the years. Sales events contributed to rise in sales. Sales rose to an all high in 2010, dipped in 2011, rose slightly in 2012 and fell to an all time low in 2013. Monthly and yearly sales trends analysis highlight that higher sales were recorded in months in which sales events happened. 

Customer segmentation and lifetime analysis shows that top spending customers are in the minority. High value customers have a consistent purchase frequuency and show a strong preference for the rock genre.
